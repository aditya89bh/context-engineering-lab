# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone,
regardless of experience level, background, or identity. We pledge to act and
interact in ways that foster an open, respectful, and constructive community.

## Our standards

Behavior that contributes to a positive environment includes:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback, especially about
  experimental method and results.
- Focusing on what is correct and well-supported by evidence, not on who is
  right.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scientific integrity

Because this is a research repository, our standards extend to how we treat
evidence:

- Do not misrepresent results, cherry-pick favorable conditions, or hide
  negative findings.
- Disclose limitations and threats to validity honestly.
- Credit prior work and ideas accurately.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the
privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.
